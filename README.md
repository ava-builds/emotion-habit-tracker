# Emotion & Habit Tracker

A calm digital bullet journal app for emotion and habit tracking.

---

## Overview

Emotion & Habit Tracker is a mobile application designed to help users track emotions, habits, energy levels, and emotional patterns through simple daily interactions.

Inspired by mood tracker bullet journals and cozy journaling experiences, the app focuses on emotional comfort, minimal interaction, and reflective self-awareness rather than productivity pressure.

The goal of this project is to create a digital space that feels calm, visually soft, and emotionally safe while helping users better understand their emotional and behavioral patterns over time.

---

## Features

### Daily Emotion Check-in
- Emotion chip selector
- Energy level slider
- Habit checklist
- Optional short notes
- Under 2-minute interaction flow

### Habit Tracking
- Custom daily habits
- Gentle consistency tracking
- Low-pressure interaction design

### Monthly Visualization
- Mood calendar
- Emotional heatmaps
- Habit consistency visualization
- Monthly emotional summaries

### Optional Cycle Tracking
Optional menstrual cycle tracking for emotional and energy pattern reflection.

---

## Design Philosophy

This project follows four core principles:

- Emotional comfort first
- Minimal and fast interaction
- Calm visual design
- Low-pressure self-reflection

The app intentionally avoids:
- aggressive notifications
- stressful productivity systems
- competitive gamification
- overwhelming dashboards
