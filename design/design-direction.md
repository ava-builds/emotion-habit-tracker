# Design Direction

## Visual Style

- Calm and emotionally soft
- Minimal interaction
- Cozy journaling aesthetic
- Clean layouts with generous spacing

---

## Inspiration

- Bullet journals
- Cozy stationery
- Minimal mobile apps
- Soft emotional interfaces

---

## Color Direction

Planned palette: Pastel palette
- muted beige
- soft lavender
- warm gray
- dusty pink
- soft blue

---

## Typography Goals

- Readable
- Soft and modern
- Emotionally calming

---

## User Experience Goals

- Reduce emotional pressure
- Encourage consistency without guilt
- Make tracking feel lightweight
- Create a calm emotional atmosphere
