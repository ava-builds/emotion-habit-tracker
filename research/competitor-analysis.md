# Competitor Analysis

## Daylio

### Strengths
- Fast mood tracking
- Simple daily interaction
- Easy to maintain

### Weaknesses
- Feels productivity-focused over time
- Limited emotional warmth
- Gamification can become repetitive

---

## Moodflow

### Strengths
- Reflection-focused experience
- Good journaling support

### Weaknesses
- Can feel overwhelming
- Requires more interaction effort

---

## Bullet Journaling

### Strengths
- Personal and emotional
- Creative and aesthetic
- Encourages reflection

### Weaknesses
- Time-consuming
- Difficult to maintain consistently

---

# Design Direction for This Project

This project aims to combine:
- the simplicity of digital tracking
- the warmth of journaling
- low-pressure interaction
- calm visual design
